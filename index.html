1. General

This Privacy Policy applies to the FishTrack application developed by Aquanest. The application is provided as a free service.

2. Information We Collect

The application may collect information that users voluntarily provide, including:

  • User uploaded photos
  • Fish type, size, weight information
  • Location and city information

All user submitted content is reviewed and approved by the application administrator before being published.

3. Use of Data

Collected data is used only for displaying fishing catch content inside the application. This data is:

  • Not sold
  • Not rented
  • Not shared with third parties for advertising purposes

4. Third-Party Services

The application uses secure third-party services such as Supabase for backend and data storage purposes, and Google AdMob for advertisements.

5. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect user data. All data transfers are encrypted.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

User-generated content may be stored as long as necessary for the operation of the application. Users may request deletion of their content.

7. Children's Privacy

The application does not target children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them. Target audience age is 12+.
